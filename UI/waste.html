Waste Management

Waste management is meant to streamline the handling transportation and disposal of various types of waste. The main aim to is to protect human health and the environment and also to emphasis minimizing of waste, cleaner production and segregation of waste at source.

We advise and assist our clients/customers to segregate waste into three categories at source as follows.

  • Rubber and plastics
  • Scrap metal
  • Paper and glass
  • 1. Scrap metal Stored in a cage and collected when full by a contracted scrap dealer for disposal.
  • 2. Waste papers Collected and stored in waste paper bins and later disposed of by waste paper dealers.
  • 3. Rubber and plastics We collect the bio degradable waste for disposal at the designated dumping site approved by NEMA.
