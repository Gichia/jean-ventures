Company Policies

Jean Ventures Services supervisory and operation team makes sure all the assignments given to us are well checked and supervised to make sure all the clients are served to the best of our ability and all the clients suggestions are discussed with the “procuring entity” and acted upon as soon as possible.

Our operation team advices our clients on areas where improvement is needed to create a conducive, attractive and hygienic working environment. Furthermore the company’s management encourage regular engagement with the clients and are always available for meetings to enhance a good working relationship.

Jean Ventures Services staff shall perform the services as per the contract and under the general guidance of the client we also notify the client of any situation beyond our obligation. The company’s staff shall be provided with appropriate personal protective clothing, equipment and identification cards by the company during the duration of this contract.

The company is also prepared and is willing to be called upon short notice any time to attend to any emergencies arising related to the services offered. A part from the supervisors on the ground, the company’s top management team will be making frequent and regular visits to the work stations to ensure efficient delivery of services is strictly adhered to.

We also ensure there is enough staff on the ground so that in cases of sicknesses, deaths and any other form of absenteeism do not disrupt the flow of work whatsoever, and where change is eminent the client is notified immediately. Finally, Jean Ventures Service providers will avail a work sheet to the client on request.

Jean Ventures Services have contracts with other organizations as shown in our company profile.

These achievements have been achieved as a result of commitment to our work in order to enhance customer satisfaction and value addition. In order to foster good customer relationship we provide quality service, uphold integrity and enhancing the spirit of partnership with all our clients. Indeed this has given us a competitive edge over other players in the industry.

Further, we know services by their very own nature are:
  • Inseparable with the provider
  • Heterogeneous and
  • Perishable

That is why we must form a partnership with a common goal of providing uncompromised service; consult frequently, dialogue, proper co-ordination and co-operation. Our management team will be available at a short notice to discuss work progress with your appointed personnel We accept constructive criticism and take advice that can help us improve our service level. We promise to be sensitive to your needs and requirements if given the opportunity to partner with you in this venture.

Jean Ventures services subscribes to the sexual harassment policy stated in the employment act 2007 cap 2, section 6, subsection (2) of the laws of Kenya. It states that:-

  • Every employee is entitled to employment that is free of sexual harassment
  • Jean Ventures Services shall take steps to ensure that no employee is subjected to sexual harassment.
  • Jean Ventures Services shall take such disciplinary measures as the employer deems appropriate against any person under the employer’s direction, who subjects any employee to sexual harassment.
  • Jean Ventures Services should explain how complaint of sexual harassment may be brought to attention of the employer.
  • Jean Ventures Services will not disclose the name of the complaint or the circumstances related to the complaint to any person except where disclosure is necessary for the purpose of investigating the complaint or taking disciplinary measures in relation thereto.
The company shall bring to the attention of each person under its direction to the policy statement required under section 6, subsection (2) of the employment Act 2007.

Jean Ventures Services recognize and appreciates the importance of health, safety and environment in the organization. Jean Ventures prominently places health safety and environment as an important subject matter in their Agenda. To translate this commitment, Jean Ventures Services shall adopt a Health, safety and environment policy which will ensure:-

  • Compliance with all relevant statutory instruments in all matters of Health, safety and Environment.
  • Appropriate consideration of all Maritime Organization (I.M.O) conventions and recommendations.
  • Formulation of rules and guidelines on matters of Health, safety and Environment and ensure compliance of the same.
  • All reasonably practicable precautions are taken to safeguard the safety and health of all employees.
  • Appropriate systems for effective communication in proving adequate information and instructions to all cadres of employees on risks to their health and safety that they may be exposed to.
  • That all working conditions and equipment / plan are safe and all working places are regularly inspected.
  • Provision of appropriate training of all employees to ensure their competence in performing their duties.
  • That environmental impact assessment is carried out on all projects that may have impact on the authority.

All the while maintaining high standards in our cleaning activities. As a company we are open to both international and local environmentally ideas. However, we stress the locally-developed knowledge since environment is by definition location-specific, we seek wherever possible, to tap into local expertise, taking into consideration the availability of requisite materials or products.

Jean Ventures Services shall provide and manage a fully integrated housekeeping, gardening, garbage collection and disposal services, including but not limited to, the upkeep of all sites and property held therein in a suitably clean and hygienic condition, seven (7) days per week.

The scope of work shall cover the supply of personnel, equipment, materials and consumables, as necessary to provide a safe and complete service to the satisfaction of the procuring entity. Further the company shall provide service of the highest possible professional standards at all times.

  • Provide onsite supervisors to maintain the highest of service with emphasis on quality, health and safety. Sites are to be reviewed regularly to ensure company standards are met.
  • Maintain a high standard of service delivery at all times.

In the event that services are not provided because of unforeseen circumstances such as strikes by employee’s riots or as a result of force majeure, the company should not hold the procuring entity liable.

The company hereby will ensure there is enough personnel on the ground so that in the unfortunate events like strikes, riots, sicknesses, deaths or any other reasons of absenteeism do not disrupt the flow of assignments whatsoever and if the company has to change, relocate and/or introduce new staff, the e procuring entity will be notified immediately. The company shall notify the procuring entity of any situation beyond its obligations.

Jean Ventures Services, under the employment act 2007 cap 2, section 5, and subsection (3) of the laws of Kenya shall promote equal opportunities in employment and strive to eliminate in any employment policy or practice contrary to that.

Jean Ventures Services shall not discriminate directly or indirectly an employee:-

  • On grounds of race, colour, sex, language, religion, political or other opinion, nationality, ethnic or social origin, disability, pregnancy, mental status or HIV Status.
  • In respect to recruitment , training, promotion, terms and conditions of employment , termination of employment or other matters arising out of the employment.
