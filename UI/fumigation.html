Fumigation & Pest Control

Pest control/ fumigation department is one of the major health oriented department in our company, and offers wide range of pest control service and consultancy. Our goal and objective as a department is to ensure clients comfort and satisfaction is attained through up to date quality service delivery. We have well trained pest control technicians and technical support staff, who understand the clients’ needs and ensure clients satisfaction takes precedence, and that the environment is kept clean and safe for the good of all living creature, human included.

Importance

Pests are of importance to us, because of the public health related hazards associated with them or the disturbance caused to our environment. Common problems associated with the pests are:-

  • Diseases such as typhoid, cholera, dysentery, viral flu, malaria plague, and rabies associated with cockroaches, mosquitos, houseflies, rodents, and bats.
  • Disease pathogen transmission (viral, bacteria or fungal)
  • Disturbance (houseflies and rodents)
  • Damage to food in stores (e.g. by the rodents and weevils)
  • Contamination of the environment
  • Property damage (rodents and termites) among others
Pest management

As a company, we offer a wide range of pest control service to our domestic, commercial, and local authority clients. Pest problems handled include:

  • Rat and mice infestations
  • Cockroach infestations
  • Wasp’s nests removal
  • Crawling insects treatment e.g. pharaoh/black/sugar ant’s and other
  • Flying insects’ treatment e.g. houseflies, beer fly moths, mosquitos, Nairobi flies and others.
  • Fleas, bedbugs, lice,
  • Bata management
  • Termites control among others.
Aim

Our main aim is to actively participate in reduction of public health hazards such as zoonotic diseases associated with these pests around as and also ensure customers get value for the money through quality service delivery.

Control Methods

We have various pest control method at our disposal. These are

  • Chemical (that involves use of pesticides)
  • Mechanical, and
  • Environmental methods.

To get the best result, we use integrated form of the above three mentioned methods, and most of all ensure the clients safety is upheld. The pesticide used by our company are thoroughly examined and approved by the PCPB as safe for use and environmentally friendly to our clients’ premise.
