Garbage Collection

We noticed that the collection and disposal of garbage will involve one trip to Mwakirunge dumping site per week.

Jean Ventures Services is licensed to offer garbage collection and disposal services by NEMA. We provide garbage collection skips/tanks that are stationed centrally at the clients’ premises to facilitate for dumping of the waste from the client’s premises. We also provide plastic bags according to the clients’ specification and requirement to facilitate for daily collection of the waste within the premises. Thorough cleaning and disinfection of dust bins is done weekly or according to the clients’ specification. The garbage skips are fumigated on quarterly interval. The staff employed to facilitate this services are appropriately dressed in blue overall uniforms, gum boots and hand gloves for safety and protection purposes. Upon collection of the garbage, it is loaded and the top covered in our garbage truck and transported to the designated dumping site approved by NEMA. I.e. Dandora in Nairobi and Mwakirunge in Mombasa where it is offloaded. Cleaning and disinfection of the truck is done on a daily basis. Jean Ventures Services is sensitive to the clients’ needs and requirements.
