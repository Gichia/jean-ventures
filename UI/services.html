Our Services

We offer wide ranging services related to general cleaning including offices and homes.

Description of Services offered

Jean Ventures Services hereby undertake to provide cleaning services to the best of our ability with the highest professional standards possible.

Identification

Our staff dress in branded uniform, our motto “Shine with Jean Ventures”.

Supervision/Service delivery

For better service delivery Jean Ventures will allocate adequate staff members and one team leader on a daily basis. Our operation management team will be passing on a weekly basis to check and monitor the services offered and ensure our clients are contented.

Tools/ Equipment

Jean Ventures Services do hereby undertake to provide the following tools and/or equipment’s in its efficient service delivery:-

  • Floor scrubbing machines
  • Soft and hard brooms
  • Window cleaner/Cobweb removers
  • Mop buckets
  • Dust pans, Toilet brushes, wiping towels
  • Squeezers/ Slashes, Pangas
  • Wheelbarrows/Spade
  • Lawnmowers/Jembes, Shauvers, Rakes
  • Sanitary bins/buckets
  • Or any other tool that may be required for the work.
