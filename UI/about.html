About Us

We are pleased to introduce Jean Ventures and General Services. Jean ventures is a registered company with the Government of Kenya.

We have engaged well trained staffs who are able to meet the cleaning needs of your company. They are competent, committed staff that specialize in general cleaning, garbage collection, and landscaping interalia working under minimum supervision. We have a fully-fledged secretariat and field officers to support and oversee our services.

Our experience in the provision of cleaning services is unmatched by none other provider in the market. Our range of services includes Cleaning, Gardening, Garbage collection and Sanitation Services, our investment in assets and geographical scope provide both the flexibility to respond to new customer needs as well as provide services from nearly all parts of the country.
